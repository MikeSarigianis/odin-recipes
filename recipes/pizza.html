Pepperoni Pizza

Description

Create a delicious homemade pizza with minimal effort! Perfect for those late-running days where you get home from work and want to cook something quick and simple without sacrificing taste!

This recipe makes a basic red pizza topped with mozzarella, pepperoni, and any extra toppings of your choice, if desired. Serving size is 2 12" pizzas. If you're not that hungry, halve the recipe to make just 1 pizza.

Ingredients

    Sauce:
  • 12oz tomato paste
  • 1 tsp dried oregano, crushed
  • 1 tsp dried basil, crushed
  • 1/2tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2tsp onion powder
  • 1/2tsp sugar
  • 1/2tsp salt
  • 1/4tsp black pepper
    Crust:
  • 3 1/4 cups all-purpose floud
  • .5oz yeast
  • 1tbsp sugar
  • 1 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 1/3 cups very warm water (120F - 130F)
  • 1/3 cup oil
    Toppings:
  • 6oz pepperoni
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella
  • Any additional toppings you want!

Steps

  1. Combine sauce ingredients + 1/2 cup water in a bowl
  2. Combine 2 cups flour with the yeast, sugar, & salt. Slowly add flour until soft dough ball can be formed
  3. Knead 4 minutes on floured surface. Knead longer if needed
  4. Divide dough in half. Pat each half into a 12-inch greased pan
  5. Preheat oven 425F. Dress crusts with sauce, moz, & pepperoni (and any additional toppings you want)
  6. Bake 18-20 minutes, until crusts are browned and cheese is bubbly
